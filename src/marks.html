SNAAP Marks

Welcome To SNAAP Marks

In order for you to be able to screen through the different service providers here on SNAAP we came up with Stamps.

Stamps is our way to show you, with a simple image, the level of knowledge and information you have on this specific vendor.

Stamps will appear alongside the app's profile and each app could have up to 4 different stamps

Are you a vendor?

Find out how to get more with SNAAP stamps!
Verification Stamp
If you see that a vendor or an app page contains the "Verified" stamp it means that account managers and site operators from SNAAP has verified that the person providing this app's information is indeed the owner of the app, or a certified employee of the company providing the service/product.
Popularity Stamp
If you see that a vendor holds the "Popular" stamp it means that his service is getting more clicks [exposure] and better reviews than others within the SNAAP directory.
Featuring Stamp
A vendor that holds "Featured" stamp in their app page is one that has purchased our unique package and will appear at the top section of the search results in the categories that best describe their service.
SNAAP Review Stamp
SNAAP "Reviewed" stamp is one of the most important stamps, as it tells you that on top of the information that the vendor published on their product/service, someone from SNAAP tried to use the app and wrote their own objective review on it.

How Can Stamps Help Vendors?

Using stamps is the quickest way to let our community know the level of your app's engagement within SNAAP.

The 4 different stamps you can get when advertising with SNAAP are: Verified, Popular, Featured, Reviewed.

You can use our stamps as a way to generate credibility within your own community by embedding the stamp into your website-thinking it ti the app-page within SNAAP.

As the design you see in our site for SNAAP stamps is meant to fit our own art-language, you can get SNAAP stamps in different colors, to fit your site's design by contacting us.
